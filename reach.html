Patna is the capital of Bihar, a state of India. Both Patna and Gaya are important city of Bihar. Both cities is well connected to New Delhi (capital of India)via road, air and train.
All distance calculation is done from these two cities.

Mahabodhi Temple, Bodh Gaya

Nearest airport: Gaya.
Nearest railway station: Gaya.
Travel time from Gaya to bodh gaya is around 30 minutes.
Gaya is well connected to Patna via train and road.
Average time to reach Gaya from Patna is around 2.5 hour to 3 hour.
Stay facility is available here, check Govt.of Bihar official site for recognized hotels.


Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Rajgir

Nearest airport: Patna (preferable).
Nearest railway station: Rajgir.
Train available between Patna to Rajgir and same for Gaya to Rajgir.
Rajgir is well connected to Patna and Gaya via train and road.
Average time to reach Rajgir from Gaya is around 2 hour.
Average time to reach Rajgir from Patna is around 2 hour to 2.5 hour.
Stay facility is available here, check Govt.of Bihar official site for recognized hotels.


Barabar Caves, Jehanabad

Gaya is the Nearest city.
Well connected to Gaya via road .
Travel time around 30 minutes from Gaya.
Must visit here. It will be half day tour
Stay facility is not available here.


Buddha Memorial Park, Patna

Situated in heart of Patna
250 meter from Patna Junction
Walking distamce of 5 mintues hardly.
Must visit here. It will be half day tour
Stay facility is available here.


Nalanda

Nearest airport: Patna (preferable).
Nearest railway station: Rajgir.
No direct train from Gaya to Nalanda.
Direct train from Patna to Nalanda. Nalanda is well connected to Patna and Gaya via road.
Average time to reach Nalanda from Gaya is around 2 hour.
Average time to reach Nalanda from Patna is around 2 hour to 2.5 hour.
Stay facility is available in Rajgir(distance is 14km), check Govt.of Bihar official site for recognized hotels.
Must visit Nalanda, if visiting Rajgir.


Ashokan Pillar, Kolhua Vaishali

Buddha Relic Stupa, Vaishali

Nearest airport: Patna (preferable).
Well connected to Patna via road.
Travel time around from Patna to Ashokan Pillar, Kolhua Vaishali is 1.25 hour.
Travel time around from Patna to Buddha Relic Stupa, Vaishali is 1.5 hour.
